Canada to Welcome Immigrants with Open Arms

Analysis by Timmy Greg , UIS Writer

Updated 12:34 PM ET, Thu February 1st, 2018

The Canadian government under the Prime Minister Justin Trudeau opened its gates to immigrants like no other previous administration. With an admission of nearly 1 million immigrants in the past 3 years and numbers are still rising. Wanting to diversify its work-force, the Canadian government eases its immigration requirements and becomes the most desirable destination for many who seek a better future for themselves.

If you think the story ends here, you’re wrong. Canada’s immigrant population has recently ranked as the world’s most prosperous and successful immigrant population. It certainly demonstrates the Canadian government’s wisdom by allowing more immigrants to legally move to Canada, but more importantly the Canadian people hospitality. Listen to this: “82 percent of the Canadian voters believe that immigrants have a positive impact on its economy, and two thirds see multiculturalism as one of Canada’s key positive features”.

Canada not only allows more immigrants into the country, it embraces them with open arms and provides them with everything they wished for; universal health care, great education, countless job opportunities and last but not least - friendly environment. Canadian authorities don’t reveal when these policies will expire, but as of now no other country provides such rich packages to its new comers like Canada, and no immigration community prospers like immigrants in Canada.
